Privacy Policy — Search Jobs Tanzania

Effective date: June 1, 2026

Search ("we", "our", "us") operates the Search Jobs Tanzania mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our app and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you register or use the app:

  • Account information: username, first name, last name, phone number, and optional address.
  • Profile information: bio, date of birth, gender, profile photo, region.
  • Professional information: work experience, education history, skills, and CV files you choose to upload.
  • Usage data: job searches, saved jobs, and job applications submitted through the app.
  • Device information: device type and OS version for crash reporting and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To match you with relevant job listings based on your region, skills, and preferences.
  • To allow employers to find discoverable seeker profiles (only when you opt in).
  • To send job alert notifications based on your saved criteria.
  • To improve the app and fix technical issues.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your profile is only visible to employers if you explicitly enable the "Discoverable" setting. When you apply for a job, your name and phone number are shared with the employer who posted the job.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers in the cloud. We use HTTPS for all data transmission. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time by contacting us.

Permissions We Request

  • Camera / Photo Library: Used only to upload a profile photo. We never access your gallery without your action.
  • Storage: Used to read and upload CV files (PDF/Word documents) that you select.
  • Internet: Required to connect to our servers.

We do not request location, microphone, contacts, or any other sensitive permissions.

Children's Privacy

Search is intended for users who are 16 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for discoverable profile visibility at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the effective date above.
